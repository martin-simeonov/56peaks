Name: Little Kupen / Малък Купен
Height: 2687 m
Duration: 5–6h
Distance: 12 km
Elevation Gain: +900 m
Elevation Loss: -900 m
Coordinates: 42.1830, 23.6060

Summary:
The beginning of the famous Kupen ridge, marked by steep granite and exposed sections. It introduces one of Rila’s most technical alpine traverses.
