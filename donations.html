Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Bulgarian 56 Peaks is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How will my donation be used?

100% of your donation goes directly to our programs. We operate on a lean budget so maximum funds reach the communities we serve.

Can I donate monthly?

Absolutely! Monthly donations provide consistent support for our ongoing initiatives. Contact us for recurring donation options.

Are donations secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and only process donations through secure payment gateways.
